ABOUT ORIGAMI
The Origami Story

Our expedition into education started in the year 1987, the founding year of National Model School and there has been no looking back. Our passion to culminate all the best practices and revolutionary changes has garnered our supported growth. We believe that every child is born with a talent and we are here to explore and give space for that talent to bloom in all our learners. While our learners began to excel at state and national levels, getting admissions in international universities remained a dream for them. This triggered us to start a school with an international curriculum to encourage learners to become confident, responsible, reflective, innovative, engaged and open up opportunities in international universities.
